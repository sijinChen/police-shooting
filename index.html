• Clicking the menu on the right corner, you can choose either showing all of the markers or showing none of the markers.
  • Each marker represents a police shooting data; black markers represent data that the victim's gender is male while red markers represent data that the victim's gender is female
  • By clicking either 'Under 18' or 'Over 18', you can see markers representing the corresponding information.
  • By clicking at the markers themselves, you can see the summary of the specific data.
